BiddyPhone Whitepaper

Democratising Bitcoin Mining Through Mobile Infrastructure

Version: 0.1 (Public Release)

Executive Summary

BiddyPhone is a groundbreaking project that combines custom ASIC mining, satellite communications, and mobile computing into a decentralized smartphone platform designed to serve the unbanked and off-grid population of the world. It enables users to mine Bitcoin using solar power and satellite connectivity, offering UBI and digital financial tools.

1. The Vision

"Sovereignty in your pocket."

  • Distribute Bitcoin mining rewards as daily UBI
  • Use renewable energy and satellite sync for autonomy
  • Build local financial sovereignty into every device

2. The Problem

  • 1.4 billion people remain unbanked
  • 3+ billion lack stable internet
  • Bitcoin mining is centralized and costly

3. The Solution

  • Ultra-low-power ASIC mining chip
  • Solar charging with daily energy capture
  • LEO satellite + mesh fallback connectivity
  • On-device wallet and DeFi tools

4. Technical Architecture

4.1 Hardware

  • Custom SHA-256 ASIC
  • Solar + LiPo battery
  • LEO satellite module, GSM/WiFi fallback

4.2 Software

  • BiddyOS (AOSP fork)
  • BlockSync + BiddyMiner daemons
  • UBI distribution logic

4.3 Satellite Infrastructure

  • LEO constellation with global sync
  • Mesh aggregation + rewards broadcast

5. Economic Model

5.1 UBI Distribution

  • 100 sats/day per user
  • 2,920 BTC/year global payout
  • 0.0139% of BTC supply annually

5.2 Mobile Mining Rewards

  • Region-adjusted mining payouts
  • Mesh and satellite pooling

6. DeFi Layer

  • On-device microloans
  • Lightning wallet, DEX access
  • Atomic swaps + bridge integrations

7. Regulatory & Compliance

  • Device + satellite licensing
  • AML/CFT compliance for UBI

8. Environmental Impact

  • Sub-1W mining ASICs
  • Solar power only
  • Recyclable, modular hardware

9. Risks & Mitigations

  • ASIC failure: Redundant logic, repair support
  • Network gaps: Mesh fallback + caching
  • Energy shortfall: UBI auto-adjustment

10. Roadmap

  • 2025: Simulator + UI release
  • 2026: ASIC prototype + pilot launch
  • 2027: Global rollout + DeFi MVP

11. Pilot Regions

  • Kenya – solar & mobile finance culture
  • Philippines – archipelago mesh viability
  • Ghana – regulatory and community support
